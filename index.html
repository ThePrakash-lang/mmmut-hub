About This Platform

This platform is created by and for the students of Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur, specifically for the Electrical Engineering department.

Our goal is to create a collaborative space where students can share and access study materials, previous year questions, lab manuals, and other resources to help each other in their academic journey.

This is a student initiative and not officially affiliated with the university, though we encourage all students to contribute to make this a comprehensive resource hub.

All resources are contributed by students and should be used for educational purposes only. Please respect copyright laws when sharing materials.
